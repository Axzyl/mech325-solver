Welcome!

This HTML document adds interactivity to the Fizz and Mech bibles that we all know and love.

Enter values into the text boxes, and the solver solves as much as it can with the info it has.

The solver follows the bibles very closely, making it easy to follow and show your work in the homework/exams.

Unfortunately, I have not implemented automatic table/graph lookup yet. Maybe in time for the midterm, hopefully.
